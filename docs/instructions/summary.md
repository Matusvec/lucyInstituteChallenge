Health Challenge Dataset Summaries

IQVIA
A large cluster of monthly datasets focused on pharmaceutical products prescribed over time. Files contain data on the prescriber, the product prescribed, and detailed quantity and prescription information.

Example Questions:
How are prescribers and the brand or type of drug that is prescribed related?
What is the relation between time and the type of drug prescribed?
How does the rate of the dosage of prescriptions change over time?
Malaria
These datasets are from a cluster-randomized, placebo-controlled, double-blinded clinical trial that was focused on testing the effect of spatial repellents on the malaria incidence rate in children over time in Western Kenya. Information is detailed per person and describes the cluster, key demographics, living conditions, treatment type, date of test, and test results as well as whether infections are new and the incidence rate to date.

Example Questions:
How do living conditions seem to affect malaria incidences with and without repellent?
Are new infections concentrated around specific variables?
How can clusters be grouped together to create groupings?
